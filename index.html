About me

I am visual designer working for over a decade for my own studio, Signified.

Over the years, I have specifically worked on the design of numerous websites, books layouts, public campaigns promos, research reports and other forms of digital media and print publications. I also have significant experience in the design of album covers, logos, and packaging. Having a firm academic background, I have a particular preference for qualitative research methods, though I have also limited experience with statistics.

I particularly enjoy working with non-profits and organisations that operate outside the established market constraints.

I write about design and critical theory here.

I write about music and art at Lost and found (Currently unavailable).
